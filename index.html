About Me

I help founders and small teams stay organized, ship cleaner websites, and win back time by combining disciplined Virtual Assistant operations with dependable front-end & full-stack development.

What I do

Over the past 6 years as a full-stack developer/programmer, I’ve handled many projects end-to-end—planning, building, and maintaining marketing sites, e-commerce storefronts, internal tools, knowledge bases, and reporting dashboards. I move comfortably from clean HTML/CSS/JavaScript and component-level polish to PHP/MySQL data work and light integrations, and I routinely support teams with WordPress/Shopify theme tweaks, form fixes, QA passes, and performance clean-ups that keep releases smooth and support queues quiet.

Breadth

Alongside code, I bring operator habits: inbox triage & follow-ups, reliable scheduling across time zones, KPI dashboards in Google Sheets/Excel, and SOPs, macros, and templates that make delivery faster and more consistent for everyone involved.

“Give me the messy inbox, the half-built page, or the unclear request—I’ll turn it into a checklist, ship it, and keep you in the loop.”
How I work

Short discovery, a small trial to align expectations, then a weekly rhythm with progress notes, metrics, and tidy handoffs. I document as I go, keep access organized, and communicate proactively so decisions are easy and work keeps moving. If you need someone who can switch between ops and code, turn ambiguity into action, and deliver quietly without drama—I’m your person.
